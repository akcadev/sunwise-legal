1. Who we are

SunWise (“SunWise”, “we”, “us”) is an iOS application that helps you track UV exposure, plan safe sun sessions, and estimate vitamin D synthesis. This policy explains what data the app handles and why. SunWise is operated by Ratera Yazılım ve Bilişim A.Ş.. For anything in this policy, contact us at raterateam@gmail.com.

SunWise does not require you to create an account. We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number to use the app.

3. How we use it

  • To calculate the UV index and recommend safe sun-exposure windows near you.
  • To record your sun sessions and estimate the vitamin D your body may have synthesized.
  • To draw the map and route summary for a recorded session.
  • To keep the app stable and diagnose crashes (diagnostics).
  • To understand which features are used so we can improve the app (aggregate analytics).
  • To manage your subscription and show the right paywall.

We do not use your data for third-party advertising, and we do not sell your personal information.

4. Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you grant permission, SunWise:

  • Reads your time-in-daylight and UV exposure to compare planned sessions with the daylight you actually got.
  • Writes each completed session to Apple Health as a UV-exposure sample plus a model-based vitamin D estimate.
Your Apple Health data stays in Apple Health on your device. We do not transmit it to our servers or any third party, we do not use it for advertising or marketing, and we do not share it with data brokers. You can revoke Health access at any time in Settings › Privacy & Security › Health › SunWise. The vitamin D figure is an estimate, not a medical measurement.

5. Location

SunWise uses your location to fetch local weather and UV data and to compute the best sun times near you. Your coordinates are sent to Apple WeatherKit to retrieve weather; Apple’s handling is governed by Apple’s privacy policy.

While a vitamin D session is actively recording, SunWise records a background location trace so the map and summary reflect the route you actually walked — even with the screen off. The trace stops the moment the session ends. To protect your privacy, the start and end of the trace are cropped by roughly 150 metres so the route does not reveal your exact home or destination. Traces are stored on your device. You can disable background location in Settings › Privacy & Security › Location Services › SunWise.

6. Camera, photos & face data

SunWise uses the camera for two things: to scan your skin tone, and, optionally, to let you attach progress photos to your tan journal. Every image involved — including any camera frame that captures your face during a skin-tone scan — is captured and processed entirely on your device.

Face data. When you run a skin-tone scan, the camera preview may include your face.
  • What we collect: live camera frames used only to estimate a single skin-tone value (on the Monk scale). We do not build a face template, run facial recognition, or attempt to identify or authenticate you.
  • How it is used: frames are analyzed in real time, on your device, solely to compute your skin-tone estimate.
  • Storage & retention: skin-tone scan frames are not saved as images. They are held only transiently in memory during the scan and discarded immediately afterwards; only the resulting skin-tone value is kept, on your device.
  • Sharing: face data and scan frames are never uploaded to our servers and are never shared with any third party.
  • Deletion: because no scan images are stored, there is nothing to delete; the derived skin-tone value is removed when you change it in the app or delete the app.

Tan-journal photos. Progress photos you choose to attach are saved and stored on your device, inside the app, so you can look back on them later. Unlike the transient skin-tone scan frames above, these photos are kept — they stay on your device until you choose to delete them (by removing the photo or the journal entry, or by deleting the app). They are never uploaded to our servers and never shared with any third party. You can revoke camera and photo access in iOS Settings at any time.

8. Retention

On-device data (profile, scans, journal, session history, traces) is kept until you delete it or delete the app. Deleting SunWise removes on-device data; data written to Apple Health remains in Apple Health until you remove it there. Diagnostic and analytics data held by Firebase is retained per Google’s default retention settings. Subscription records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations.

9. Your choices & rights

  • Analytics: you can opt out of analytics and diagnostics collection in the app’s settings; when disabled, SunWise stops sending events to Firebase.
  • Permissions: revoke Location, Health, Camera, Photos, or Notifications any time in iOS Settings.
  • Deletion: delete the app to remove on-device data; remove Health samples in the Health app.
  • Access / erasure requests: depending on where you live (e.g. the EU/EEA under GDPR, or California under the CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Email raterateam@gmail.com and we will respond as required by law.

10. Children

SunWise is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Security & international transfers

We rely on Apple’s on-device security and our providers’ safeguards to protect your data. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. Our providers (Apple, Google, RevenueCat, Superwall) may process data in the United States and other countries; where required, they rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers.

12. Changes & contact
